Meet The Core Team

Home/TEAM
Sandeep Menghi
Founder / Creator in Chief
Sandeep has an experience of 20 years in the industry. He has served in Motorola, McCann, and Leo Burnett at senior levels and has helped many large businesses to establish in various markets like India, SWA & Canada. In the past 9 years he has produced some superior quality Ad films / digital content / audio content at scale and economy for more than 15 brands contributing to their success stories. Driven by the belief of Directing one’s own destiny, he believes that realism, dreams and quirkiness are 3 key ingredients for any content to create an impact on the audience.
Saurabh Malhotra
CEO - India & MENA
Saurabh has 34 years of rich cross-border multicultural growth-driven business leadership & organisational development experience in various geographies. With the global exposure in 6 organisations, he is an ace business strategist and is a key Strategic think tank for Content Factory. He is the catalyst in making content factory future ready with the right technology and alliances.
Ravinder Siwach
Director, Writer, Poet
Ravinder is a seasoned writer and poet in love with Cinema, Literature and Music since his college days. Back in the day in mid-nineties, he has been invited to read his poems on All India radio and Doordarshan. He was also one of the youngest poets to be part of a Hindi poetry anthology that included top contemporary poets of the likes of Manglesh Dabral, Laltu, Satyapal Sehgal amongst others. At mere age of 21, he was also writing book reviews for ‘The Tribune’ – a top english daily in India. His career in advertising saw him writing for brands as well as social causes and saw him working with likes of Prasoon Joshi who still recalls how just his narration of two poems was enough for him to say, “Come work with me.”
Asadullah Khan
Technical Director
Asad made his bones in the industry as an entrepreneur, AV Head, Director, Producer & Actor over his 35+ years of experience working with MNCs, SMEs & hyperlocal market players across both public & private sectors. In his very first venture in 1987 as a co-founder of Act One Theatre Group his inaugural production, "Pagla Ghoda," set the stage for a string of successful plays guided by the visionary N K Sharma. The play "Netwa" served as a launching pad, featuring talented stars like Manoj Bajpayee, Piyush Mishra, Gajraj Rao, and Ashish Vidhyarthee, among others. From captivating theatre audiences to owning Picture This production house, one thing that remained common in all his endeavors is his technical prowess & ability to execute creative solutions while controlling cost heads and delivery times. His work has been validated time and again including prominent recognition at Cannes Film Festival amongst other awards.

Ayesha Mehra

Executive Producer

Ayesha has over 10 years of experience in advertising and production where she worked with some of the most prestigious brands like DS Group, PepsiCo, Canon, Mastercard, Aircel, Dainik Jagran, JK Tyre, PNB Metlife, DLF Mall of India, Indian Badminton League while working with McCann & Dentsu. She is a go-getter and puts her heart and soul into delivering superior quality ad films and digital content. She has the ability to perform in very high pressure situations with a positive attitude. Her passion for film production and eye for minutest details sets her apart from any other producer.

Sujit Kumar Singh

Senior Producer

Sujit is an amalgamation of creativity and passion who posseses an extraordinary ability to balance logical thinking and artistic expression. He can wear multiple hats at any given point of time and handle any department during the entire production process. Very strongly associated with various theatre groups like Asmita, Third Bell, Stage Prayers from past 11 years, he has also worked at the ground level and performed in more than 500 street plays.

Dinesh
