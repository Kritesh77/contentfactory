ABOUT US

Home/ABOUT US

We are 9 years old but we see that as 9x365x24x60 sec doing what we love to do

Our Journey started in 2014 as an audio production house based in New Delhi, India from a small office in South Delhi. And the faith of our clients gave us confidence to fast explore the video space in 2017.

And in that time, we have spent 19,000 hours on the job & produced 1,62,000 Sec worth of content

In the 9 years of our existence, a lean team of 14 people that forms the core has developed audio content for 18 brands & video content for 10 brands across 9 categories, 9 Indian languages and 5 international languages with our clients engaging with us on multiple projects over the time, enabling us to delight them with premium quality content at scale and economy across India, Canada & MENA regions.

While people loved the work, the ones who judged gave us these awards

Over the past 9 years we have got 7 international awards. We were just as excited in 2017 when we bagged our first bronze in Adfest in the radio category for iXigo as we were in 2022 when we got the best in the category award at The Summit International Awards for a multiculture adfilm that we produced for Telus Canada.

Our world

Our Inhouse team of 1 director, 1 techincal director, 2 producers, 2 sound engineeds, 2 editors, 3 content writers, 3 music directors, and access to global freelance talent of more than 100 folks have made it possible for us to engage with VIVO, Suzuki, Amazon, Samsung, Toyota, Telus Canada, ixigo, McDonalds, Nestle, Nokia, Dabur, Sprite, Bajaj Finserv, Radio City, Burger Singh, Dermaclinix, Perfetti, Dettol, Horlicks, Harpic and Unicef time and again.
